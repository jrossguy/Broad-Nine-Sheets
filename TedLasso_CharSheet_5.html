First Impression — Passive
NPCs she meets for the first time start one disposition tier higher than they otherwise would. Not automatically friends — just pre-warmed. He doesn't make friends instantly; he makes the road shorter.

Pass a Healing Charge, Repair Kit, or Adrenaline Shot to any ally within 5ft as a Free Action. Normally passing a consumable costs 1 Movement — this ability waives that cost entirely.
